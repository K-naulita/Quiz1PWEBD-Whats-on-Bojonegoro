Your trip isn't complete without trying Bojonegoro's local food

Ledre Sego Buwuhan Gethuk Lindri Nasi Flambe Wader Kali Sego Mawut

Kue Ledre

A sweet and crispy snack made from banana and flour, rolled into thin wafers. It's a signature treat from Bojonegoro and popular as a travel souvenir.

Sego Buwuhan

A traditional rice dish served with various side dishes during communal feasts. It reflects the togetherness and cultural heritage of Bojonegoro people.

Kue Gethuk Lindri

A colorful cassava-based cake with a soft texture and sweet flavor. Often enjoyed with grated coconut, it's a nostalgic traditional snack.

Nasi Flambe

A unique rice dish cooked with spices and then flamb√©ed, giving it a smoky aroma and rich flavor. It's both a culinary treat and a visual experience when served.

Wader Kali

Small river fish fried until golden and crispy, served with sambal or warm rice. It's a local favorite for its simple yet savory taste.

Sego Mawut

A flavorful combination of fried rice and fried noodles, mixed with vegetables and spices. This dish is known for its rich taste and filling portion.
